Bountiful Davis Art Center wecomes NOVA Chamber Concert

Sat. May 14th, 7PM

Join NOVA for a special fundraiser concert  at the Bountiful Davis Arts Center featuring Utah Symphony Concertmaster Ralph Matson in recital, with NOVA Artistic Director and pianist Jason Hardink and a very special guest appearance by internationally acclaimed soprano Celena Shafer. The program features music by Brahms, Schumann, Strauss, Gershwin, Bolcom, and Joplin. All proceeds benefit both NOVA and BDAC.



Seating is limited to 100.

Saturday May 14, 2016 @ 7:00 PM
