Webster defines Bountiful like this:

boun·ti·ful - ˈboun(t)əfəl

adjective: giving or providing many desired things

 

Not a bad aspiration for a town or an outlook on life. As a life-long advocate for acheiving both, I've posted some of my own definations of Bountiful and/or bountiful below. I hope that you will take a few moments to do the same.

Why? Because what qualifies as bountiful to you, ordinary as it may seem, will likely offer hope and inspiration to somebody else who is having a difficult time finding the bounty in their own life.
