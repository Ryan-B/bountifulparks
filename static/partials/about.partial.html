Twin Hollows Uniting Neighbors

The Bountiful City Council anticipated some level of opposition to a local developer's recent request for endorsement of a proposed public/private landswap. So they called for a public hearing to gauge community response to the idea. What they didn't anticipate was the formation of a determined neighborhood coalition called Twin Hollows United to insure that their foothills remained open and undeveloped.

Bountiful Business

Creating Dancers, Friends and Memories

Growing up wedged between two brothers, I had plenty of rough and tumble, Pirates and cowboy play. There was a plethora of muddy handprints and grass stained shorts. In fact often I came in from playing outside being far more dirty than either of my brothers. I needed a little more frill and lace in my life (like most little girls thrive on) not to mention some gracefulness. And so when I was six years old my parents enrolled me in ballet classes at the Bountiful School of Ballet.

Bountiful Schools

BHS Welcomes All Fools For Jazz

Nobody likes to be called a fool and yet hundreds of people turned up at Bountiful High School on the evening of April 25th knowing that's exactly what they'd be called. Most were called that last year and they'll likely be called that next year too. Fools For Jazz is the annual fund raiser for the Bountiful High Jazz Bands and many Bountiful families make a tradtion out of singing and dancing and eating like fools year after year.

Bountiful Events

BDAC Teaming with NOVA for Benefit Chamber Concert

The Bountiful Davis Art Center and the NOVA Chamber Concert Series are joining together to bring fine Chamber Music and Art to downtown Bountiful. Join both arts groups on May 14 for a special fundraiser concert at the Bountiful Davis Arts Center featuring Utah Symphony Concertmaster Ralph Matson in recital, with NOVA Artistic Director and pianist Jason Hardink and a very special guest appearance by internationally acclaimed soprano Celena Shafer. The program features music by Brahms, Schumann, Strauss, Gershwin, Bolcom, and Joplin. All proceeds benefit both NOVA and BDAC.
